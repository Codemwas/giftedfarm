INTRODUCTION

Gifted Farm Enterprises is a dynamic enterprise that specializes in 4 sectors i.e. agri-business, retail and warehousing, eco-tourism and professional services. Our management team shares extensive knowledge, experience, and expertise in their fields of work. We do offer our valued clients low risk services in an efficient and timely manner. We believe in hands on visible management for optimal performance, incident free and on time delivery in the interest of the clients and contracts personnel. Since the business inception in 2020, GFE has successfully initiated numerous business ventures in various sectors.

FLEXIBILITY IN APPROACH

GFE management is available to work in partnership with companies/organizations or their supply chain, to fit in with specific projects requirements. GFE strives to minimize disruption to the clients’ marketing process. GFE provides complete marketing management services, which includes; assistance with the design of the marketing strategy, clients data profiling, warehousing, distribution and training in integrated business management systems.

RISK MANAGEMENT

GFE will continue to cultivate a sustainable quality oriented and risk managed controlled culture throughout the organization and the industry. The company believes in empowering (training) its people, enabling them to predict, prevent and control any incident and non conformal events. Continuous quality, risk management systems and controls are maintained as this forms part of the innovative approach in managing the wellbeing of our people and our business.
